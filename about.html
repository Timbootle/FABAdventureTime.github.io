Who we are

Adventures built with care, experience, and local insight.

FAB Adventure Time is a small, detail‑driven travel planning team focused on national parks, coastal escapes, and scenic road trips. We build trips that feel personal, not packaged.

Our approach

We don’t try to cover the entire world. Instead, we specialize in a handful of regions we know deeply — the Florida Keys, the Great Smoky Mountains, and the national and state parks of the Dakotas, Wyoming, Utah, and Arizona.

That focus lets us give you honest recommendations, realistic timing, and itineraries that actually match how people travel.

What makes us different

  • We design trips around your pace, not a template.
  • We prioritize scenic routes, timing, and logistics.
  • We know the viewpoints, the quiet spots, and the “don’t miss” moments.
  • We keep things simple, clear, and easy to follow.

Why we do this

Travel should feel exciting, not overwhelming. Our goal is to remove the stress of planning so you can focus on the experience — the sunrise over the mountains, the quiet of a trail, the glow of a Keys sunset.
